Registration is free and quick. You will only need your Goolge account or email address and complete the registration form to do it.

  • Name - your name.
  • Country
    • If the type is person - select the country where the person comes from
    • If the type is company - select where the company is registered.
  • Email - the email used to login. This is also the default email where the email notifications will be sent. You can change the notification email at any time.
  • Password - password for the account
